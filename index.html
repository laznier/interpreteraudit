Core Services

Post-Hearing Transcript Audit & Expert Declaration

Line-by-line comparison of the official English transcript against the original Russian audio recording. Designed for motions to reopen, BIA appeals, and petitions for review where interpretation error is at issue.

  • Comprehensive error log with timestamps and severity classification
  • Expert affidavit/declaration suitable for filing
  • Summary of material errors affecting credibility findings or due process
  • Consultation on framing ineffective assistance of interpreter claims
Live Courtroom Interpreting

Federally certified consecutive and, where appropriate, simultaneous interpreting for immigration and federal court proceedings.

Real-Time Monitoring

On-site or remote monitoring of interpreted hearings with contemporaneous notes and a post-hearing summary for counsel.

Deposition & Consultation Interpretation

Confidential interpretation for attorney–client meetings, witness preparation, depositions, and settlement discussions.

Interpreter & Founder

Interpreter Audit is a one-person practice. Every audit, declaration, and live assignment is handled personally by Zimma, a Federally Certified Russian court interpreter with significant experience in immigration, federal, criminal, and civil proceedings.

The business is veteran-owned and operated: Interpreter Audit is supported by her husband, an active-duty U.S. service member, and built around the same values that govern military service—precision, integrity, and accountability.

For each case, Zimma works directly with counsel to understand the theory of appeal, prioritize the record segments that matter most, and deliver a clear, well-documented analysis that fits into your briefing strategy.
